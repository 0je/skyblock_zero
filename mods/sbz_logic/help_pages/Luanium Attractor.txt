<bigger>Luanium Attractor</bigger>

It attracts/repels meteorites depending on the value its given.
You can send it <mono>{ attract = 10 }</mono> (with the attract value being -10 to 10) to attrasct meteorites 10x the force of a normal attractor
You can send it <mono>{ type = "auto", attract = 10 }</mono> to automatically attract the meteorites, though it needs to be connected to a power network.
