<bigger>Getting Started</bigger>
Craft:
1 linking unpgrade
1 luacontroller linker (or multiple for longer ranges)
1 lua controller
1 basic editor disk
Optional: Any devices that you want to use

Instructions:
1) Place the luacontroller to a switching station network
2) Punch the basic editor disk into the luacontroller
3) Go to the inventory tab, put in your linking upgrade
4) With your link tool, press aux1+shift+right click to link the luacontroller with the linking tool
5) From that, if you have any devices that you want the luacontroller to use, right click the devices to give them a name, then click to link them 
- one name can have multiple links
- "linking" again will make the link go away
